What is a NFT?
‍

NFTs (Non Fungible Tokens) are used to represent ownership of unique items. Nearly everything can be made into NFTs, art, literature, videos, digital real estate, just about anything!

What is Staking?
‍

NFT staking refers to the action of locking up NFTs on a project's platform to receive staking rewards and other privileges. This allows NFT holders to earn a passive income while still maintaining ownership of their NFTs.
